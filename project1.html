Les Sables Roses de Louis Vuitton est un parfum Ambre Floral pour homme et femme. Les Sables Roses a été lancé en 2019. Le nez derrière ce parfum est Jacques Cavallier.

Sauvage Eau de Parfum by Christian Dior is an Oriental Fougere fragrance for men. The top note is Bergamot. The middle notes are Sichuan Pepper, Lavender, Star Anise and Nutmeg. The base notes are Ambroxan and Vanilla. Sauvage Eau de Parfum was launched in 2018. The nose behind this fragrance is Francois Demachy

Boss Bottled Infinite by Hugo Boss is a Woody Aromatic fragrance for men. Boss Bottled Infinite was launched in 2019. Top notes are Apple, Cinnamon, Sage and Mandarin Orange; middle notes are Lavender, Rosemary and Patchouli; base notes are Sandalwood and Olive Tree.
